About Us

At DentNord, we specialize in bringing digital technology into modern dentistry. Our focus is on precision, innovation, and supporting dentists with advanced digital solutions.

With expertise in implant planning, surgical guides, and digital workflows, we help clinics improve treatment outcomes and patient satisfaction. Our goal is to make dentistry more predictable, efficient, and accessible with state-of-the-art tools.

Our Services

  • Digital Implant Planning – Accurate 3D planning for predictable implant placement using the latest imaging and software tools.
  • Surgical Guide Design – Custom-designed guides that ensure safe, precise, and minimally invasive implant surgery.
  • Digital Prosthetic Design – CAD/CAM solutions for crowns, bridges, and implant-supported restorations with excellent fit and aesthetics.
  • Consultancy & Training – Guidance for clinics adopting digital workflows, including surgical guide usage and advanced implantology.
  • Patient-Centered Solutions – Tools designed to reduce treatment time, improve comfort, and deliver long-lasting results.
